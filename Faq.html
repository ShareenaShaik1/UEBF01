FAQ
    1.How do farmers use electricity in agriculture?

    A.Farmers use electricity for various purposes in agriculture, such as operating irrigation pumps, running machinery like tractors and harvesters, and powering storage facilities like cold storage units.

    2.What are the benefits of using electricity in agriculture?

    A.Electricity enables efficient operations by reducing manual labor, increasing productivity, ensuring timely irrigation, and facilitating the use of modern farming equipment for improved yield and quality.

    3.How do farmers ensure efficient electricity usage on their farms?

    A.Farmers can ensure efficient electricity usage by investing in energy-efficient equipment, maintaining their machinery regularly, utilizing renewable energy sources like solar panels, and adopting smart irrigation systems.

    4.What are the challenges faced by farmers in accessing electricity?

    A.Challenges include unreliable power supply, high electricity costs, insufficient infrastructure in remote areas, and the initial investment required for installing electricity-dependent equipment.

    5.How does electricity usage impact crop production?

    A.Electricity usage impacts crop production positively by enabling timely irrigation, mechanized planting and harvesting, controlled environmental conditions (through heating or cooling), and better storage facilities to reduce post-harvest losses.
